Purpose of the CHPQA Programme

The CHPQA programme has been established for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate the electronic submission of applications, information, and reports by Responsible Persons involved in Combined Heat and Power (CHP) schemes.
  • To enhance communication between Responsible Persons, Assessors, Certifiers, Auditors and Administrators regarding CHP systems.
  • To ensure compliance with relevant regulations governing CHP systems in the UK.

  All information submitted through the CHPQA programme will be transmitted electronically to the appropriate Assessor.
